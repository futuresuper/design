Fossil fuels

Future Super does not invest in any company that extracts, burns, relies on or provides significant services to the fossil fuel industry.

We preference

  • Clean energy
  • Renewables technology
  • Sustainable agriculture
  • Social impact
  • Clean energy

Renewables technology

Future Super preferences companies that produce technology for renewable energy.
