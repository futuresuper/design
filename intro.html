Invest

The super industry’s purpose is to invest and grow Australia’s retirement savings.

But super funds have consistently invested money in fossil fuels and other harmful industries that have set us on a path towards climate change.

Future Super offers a better way to invest super for your future.

We grow retirement savings by investing in a future free from climate change and inequality.

The way we achieve this relies on what we seek from your investments...

...and what we exclude from your investments.
